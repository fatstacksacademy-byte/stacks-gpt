- Strict rule applied: institution's own official page is the only proof. Where WebFetch hit 403/Cloudflare/JS-only/ECONNREFUSED, fell back to WebSearch with `allowed_domains` = the institution's own domain. Aggregator text used for discovery only.
- Wayback/web.archive.org not used (blocked in this environment).
- No bonuses invented: institutions with a card but no advertised signup bonus are recorded as card bonus = 0; expired = expired (never inferred live).
- Card side was already deep (`georgia.ts`, ~22 cards) — this pass prioritized deposit and confirmed no new advertised card signup bonuses exist at the un-cataloged regional issuers checked.
