# Card Rankings — 4-Video Series (script + locked lists)

Generated from the live catalog via `npm run yt:cards` (add `--recognizable` to lock to
known brands). **Method, applied to all four:** every point valued at **1¢** (cash floor),
**no statement credits**, **no airline/hotel/restricted currencies** (1¢ overstates them).
- **Series A "Most Money"** = signup bonus + rewards earned hitting the minimum spend − annual fee.
- **Series B "Best Keeper"** = ongoing category rewards − annual fee, on a ~$3,300/mo profile.

**Tag key (say these out loud — the honesty is the differentiator):**
`[UP TO]` variable/targeted offer, most people get less · `[HIGH SPEND]` big minimum spend ·
`[FEE]` notable annual fee · `[VERIFY]` new/unconfirmed record, confirm before claiming on camera.
