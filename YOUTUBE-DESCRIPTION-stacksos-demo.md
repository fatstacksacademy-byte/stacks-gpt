**FREE** — find any of the ~1,400 bonuses on the site (checking, savings, and credit cards, including state-specific and regional offers) and track each one step by step, with the real requirements, deadlines, and cooldowns. It checks every bonus against your current savings APY so you know if it's worth doing. It already beats any spreadsheet.

**PRO** — $10/mo or $100/yr. Includes the savings sequencer (ranks by effective APY for your balance), the spending/credit-card optimizer (beta), and the 0% APR optimizer (beta). *Founding subscribers stay locked in at their original rate.*
