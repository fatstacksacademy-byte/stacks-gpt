## Why these win (the mechanics)
From the savings-niche teardown + 2026 title-formula data, every breakout combines three things:
1. **Contrarian reframe of the default** — the default (your savings account / your bank) is recast as the *loser*. "Why" beats "How to" in curiosity niches. The biggest winners aren't "here's a good savings account," they're "savings accounts are the *wrong* answer — do this instead."
2. **A specific number** — "Earn 4.3% Instead," "$50,000," "$10,000+." Precise numbers read as credible; round/vague ones don't.
3. **Personal stake / proof** — "I moved *my own* money." First-person ("I"/"you") + on-screen proof drives CTR and retention far past generic explainers.

Avoid: tier-lists / "best of 2026" roundups (they dilute the curiosity hook and underperform single-protagonist deep-dives), and undated rates ("4.3%" with no month ages out fast).
