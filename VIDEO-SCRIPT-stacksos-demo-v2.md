# Stacks OS Demo v2 — the "guided" re-record (funnel-optimized)

Fixes the v1 leaks: **hook first**, value before price, one clear free/Pro line, benefit-led
(not a UI tour), the 2–3 "wow" moments dramatized, a single clear CTA, and **zero
self-undercutting** (no "I rushed this," no apologies, no on-screen bugs). Target **8–10 min**.

Voice: warm, "beautiful people / you guys" — talk *to* one person. Lines in **>** are spoken;
**[SCREEN]** is what's on screen. Record screen first, VO after. Don't read it word-for-word —
these are the beats; riff in your voice.

> ⚠️ Before you roll: log into your REAL account (real numbers = the whole credibility play),
> confirm the live Pro price ($10/mo · $100/yr), and DON'T narrate any half-finished feature as
> if it's done. If something's beta, say it once, briefly, and move on.

[SCREEN: the ranked Pro queue, top to bottom]

**WOW #1 — it's personalized (dramatize the re-rank):**
> "And it's built around YOUR money. Watch this — I'll drop the paycheck down to two hundred bucks…

[SCREEN: change the paycheck number → queue visibly re-orders]

**WOW #2 — APY-aware savings (the crazy number):**

[SCREEN: savings sequencer · let the 73% APY sit on screen a beat · toggle "rank by amount"]

**WOW #3 — turnability (nobody else tracks this):**
> "And this is my favorite part: it remembers what you've already done. I closed a Wells Fargo bonus
